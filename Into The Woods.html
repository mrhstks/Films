  • Into The Woods

Into the Woods, a theatrical musical turned film, was released in 2014 starring big names
like Anna Kendrick, James Gorden, Emily Blunt, and Meryl Streep. This movie took the
musical world by storm. Everything about this movie made me fall in love with musicals all over again
This movie is a colorfully fun adaption of some of our most common fairytales with an emphasizes
on the weakness of humanity. Stories such asLittle Red Riding Hood, Goldilocks, and Cinderella,
 all show up in this film. The creators of this piece of artwork perfectly combined important
concepts while keeping the attitude of the film playful and open-hearted. I remember singing
the songs from this movie in High School. Its impact on me will never be forgotten.

The Inside Out Series released its debut film in 2015. This hidden gem, absolutely changed
my life and touched a part of my being that had been left untapped for a long time.
The first film follows eleven year old Riley as she navigates moving to a new city and all
the uncertainties that come with it. Her main emotions: Joy, Anger, Sadness, Disgust, and Fear,
control her everyday movements and moments as she navigates the world around her.
The second film caught up with Riley again when she turned 13 years old. We saw her dealing with new emotions
as she navigated the complex nature of being a teenager. This film series has absolutely changed my life.
I could watch these movies over and over again and cry every single time. It's no longer just a
movie, it's a therapy session. This groundbreaking cartoon was able to simplify a majority of complex emotions
in a way little kids could understand. Disney and Pixar didn't just create a movie, they created a movement.

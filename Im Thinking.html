Im Thinking of Ending Things, released in 2020, is an offbeat, cerebral
exploration of the roles that fantasies play in our everyday lives
especially for those who struggle with mental health issues.
A young decides that it's time to take his girlfriend to visit his family
farm and meet his parents. Throughout their journey, they encounter
a series of strange events including: one of the main characters's name
changing throughout the film, timelines being distorted, and outfits suddenly changing
the whole movie takes place in one night but makes you feel as if you have witnessed
many lifetimes. I've seen so many movies but the beautiful way that this movie was crafted
impacted me on a different level. The color pallete, the poetic dialogue, and the stunning
use of sound. Surprisingly, this is one movie that I can watch over and over again.
